INFO PANEL

Controls:

  • Arrows - move and rotate tank body
  • A, D - rotate tank turret
  • S - Fire (gun slot 1)
  • W - Fire (gun slot 2)
  • SHIFT - Use collected powerup
  • TAB - Hold to show statistics in a game
  • CTRL - Hide or show this panel
  • F11 - Toggle fullscreen
  • ENTER - chat

Game modes:

ARENA - Your goal is to strike as many kills in a row as you can. Every kill heals your tank completely and increases maximum amout of your hitpoints (up to maximum value). When you die, your killing streak ends and your maximum health is reduced to initial value - you start from the beginning. You can join or leave this game whenever you want to. If the game is full, new Arena is created when you try to join. To go back to menu, click on the button in the top-right corner.
